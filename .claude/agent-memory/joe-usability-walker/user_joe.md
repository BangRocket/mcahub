Joe runs a small survival Minecraft server for 6 friends. He has no programming background and no patience for developer vocabulary. His core job-to-be-done is: someone blew up my base last night — who did it, where, and when? Secondary jobs: let a friend in, see what the world looked like before the grief. He will give up if a task requires more than 3 clicks or encounters unexplained jargon. He may be red-green colorblind (affects ~8% of men). He is likely on a desktop but the app's max-width is 960px so mobile matters.
